# Manual Approve Posted Purchase Invoices

In Business Central, the approval flow for purchase invoices is placed before posting by default. This is intended to be logical, but from an accounting perspective, it is a problem.

## Introduction

After all, a received invoice is a legal obligation. It belongs in the general ledger --- regardless of whether someone has clicked 'approved' internally.

If you place approval before posting:

* Your liabilities are incomplete
* Your cash flow forecast is incorrect
* You are managing based on figures that do not reflect reality

That is no minor inconvenience. That is managing with a distorted mirror.

The solution for this is this app, **Posted Invoice Approval**.

The invoice is indeed posted immediately --- and is therefore visible in your reporting. However, release for payment only occurs after approval. Accountability and authorization are neatly separated, exactly as auditors and accountants intend.

No more messy month-end closing. No more incomplete accounts payable records. Just: figures that add up.
